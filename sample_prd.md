# PRD: Procurement Workflow Automation Platform

## Problem
Mid-market finance and operations teams rely on manual purchase approval workflows that are slow and error-prone.

## Goal
Build a SaaS platform that automates request intake, policy checks, approval routing, and spend analytics.

## Users
- Finance managers
- Procurement specialists
- Department approvers

## Core Features
- Intake form for purchase requests
- Policy rule engine for auto-approval/auto-rejection suggestions
- Multi-step approval workflows
- Dashboard for cycle time, bottlenecks, and category spend

## Constraints
- MVP in 8 weeks
- SOC2-ready architecture
- Integrations with Slack and email
